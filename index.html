How it works

Set it up once.
Recover continuously.

No new workflows. No additional staff. ClaimFlow connects to your existing system and runs quietly in the background.

Step 01

Connect your system

We integrate with your existing practice management or EHR software. Setup takes less than 30 minutes — we handle everything.

Step 02

Denials are identified

Every claim response is automatically reviewed. Denials are classified, evaluated for recovery potential, and prioritized — no manual review needed.

Step 03

Appeals written and submitted

Compliant appeal letters are generated per payer, per denial type, and submitted through the correct channels. Every submission is tracked.

Step 04

You get paid

Recovered funds go directly to your practice. ClaimFlow takes 20% of what was recovered — nothing more, nothing ever upfront.
